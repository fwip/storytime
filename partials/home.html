Welcome to StoryTime

Getting Started

StoryTime is a simple way to make and play interactive stories. To get a feel for how things work, check out the Writer tab. Once you've written a story (or imported one from a friend), try it out in the Game tab.

Contributing

The full source to StoryTime is hosted on Github. If something's broken, unintuitive, or if you've just got a new idea that you'd like to see, let me know at the issues page, and I'll take a look at it.
